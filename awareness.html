Security Awareness

May 14, 2017    Presentation Security Awareness High School



 About two weeks ago, I made a presentation about security. It was a kind of awareness stuff within the context of internet week.

There were a bunch of topic in my agenda.


  • Introduction to Internet
  • Introduction to Cyber Security
  • Hacker/Cracker/
  • Why do people hack?

  • Hacktivism
  • Blackhat/Grayhat/Whitehat/Redhat -ofc red;)-
  • Tor Network(breaking the deep web approach)
  • Penetration Testing
  • Phase of Penetration Testing


 That was alright but while I was talking about the danger which we could be encounter some people thought that it wasn't real. Of course I had had been waiting for a long time to make some demonstration:) ( actually exploiting some vulnerabilities to gain some $hell :p ) And in the sequel, we made a Q&A part and I think we corrected the false facts of security. It's like a sort of ironing out the kinks on people's mind;)

 All in all, I am thinking that it was profitable for everyone in the conference hall.
